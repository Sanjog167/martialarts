Summertime is FUN TIME! Our Summer Camp offers children an exciting, productive summer of fun and learning.

Our Summer Camp is great for children between the ages of 4 and 13 years old.

Camp days include traditional Taekwondo training, ATA Extreme Martial Arts training, physical games, movies, arts & crafts, and other constructive activities... truly a summer to remember!

Camps are offered all summer long. Spaces fill quickly - be sure to call or ask for details if you are interested as we often have great opportunities to save for those who enroll early.
